What it does

ContentCam is a focused camera app for people who publish to multiple platforms — shoot once, get both aspect ratios.

Two formats, one take

Every recording produces a portrait 9:16 file and a landscape 16:9 file at the same time, from the same moment.

Full field of view, both ways

Single-lens mode captures from the 4:3 sensor, so the landscape crop keeps full horizontal FOV — not just a stretched portrait crop.

Dual-lens mode

On supported iPhones, the wide and ultra-wide cameras run together for genuinely different portrait and landscape framing.

Creator workflow

Project / scene / take organisation, automatic file naming, and a side-by-side review after every take — save, delete, or share.

Manual controls

Tap to focus, drag to set exposure, pinch to zoom, switch lenses, torch toggle. Familiar, like the native camera.

On-device, private

No account, no login, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. Everything stays on your phone.

Privacy

ContentCam collects nothing.

The app does not request, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. There is no account, no login, no telemetry, and no third-party SDKs. It only asks for Camera, Microphone, and Photo Library (add-only) permissions — all required to record video and save your takes.
